"To systematically map and characterize India's geological framework; to discover and responsibly quantify the nation's mineral and energy resources (except oil & gas); to assess geohazards and address climate change challenges; and to disseminate geospatial data openly to drive innovation and informed decision-making for all stakeholders." Simplified Mission:

[cite_start]

Our job is to map all of India's land, find new minerals (like Lithium), predict earth-hazards like landslides, and share this information freely with the public and businesses. [cite: 44]
